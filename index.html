Revolutionizing Education

Since the onset of education, mankind has philosophised the ideology of “one-size fits all”. This approach turned out to be brilliant for churning out factory workers during the industrial revolution and uplifting the economy. Over the years, this approach has not been updated to match our times of rapid technology growth, innovation and the coming of the age of information. Rather, it has been engraved into our educational system such that we can’t imagine any other system into existence to educate the young minds of today’s generation. The major problem is that we, now, live in an era that rewards creativity and innovation. Today’s need is to produce thinkers who will shape the society and solve complex problem.

We, at Zaryiya, believe that each individual is unique: they have different aspirations, perspectives, learning style, and levels of creativity. Everyone deserves an Educational system which prioritises their needs above any other and provides specifically tailored curriculum which makes the individual achieve their true potential through a rigorous yet immersive pathway environment.

Through Zariya, we want to create a passionate, mission driven society, where each individual contributes to the economy through innovation and discovery of new knowledge.

Personalised Learning

Our algorithm utilises your interests, personality, learning style and tailors an intricately personalised educational experience. Such an experience helps you recognize and mend your weaknesses, capitalise on your strenghts, and build a strong passion in the area of your interest.

Community

An educational experience is incomplete without classmates, who challenge us and help us grow. With Zariya, you have access to students across the world who like you have taken an initiative to accelerate their education. We organize quizzes, contests and innovation battles, in which you can team up with your fellow classmates and enrich your experience.

Affordability

We believe that no one should be denied a quality education. As a result, we run on a low cost subscription-based model. This enables student to pay according to their usage and keeps the overall costs low. Along with this, we regularly give out merit-based scholarships to qualified students

Accessibility

Our product is optimized to run on most network connections, and can be accessed by anyone from anywhere.

Integrating Technology

To increase practicality in the cirriculum and ability of students to apply concepts to real life, we use augmented and virtual reality to power our content. This helps us to create an amazing technology stack that uses data analytics and robust user adaptation models to aid our students.

Mentoring

We put massive emotional investment in each of our student. Our main goal is to set them up for success and global contribution by helping them find their passions and aspirations. Therefore, each student is assigned a personal mentor who provides them a emotional and professional support to reach their goals in life.
